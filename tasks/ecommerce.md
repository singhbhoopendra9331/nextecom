# Recommended MVP (for your Next.js + Prisma project)

Based on your current stack, I'd prioritize these features first:

1. Authentication & Roles (Admin/Customer)
2. Product CRUD with variants and inventory
3. Categories, Brands, and Tags
4. Media Library (already implemented)
5. Product Search, Filters, and Sorting
6. Shopping Cart
7. Checkout
8. Payment Integration
9. Order Management
10. Customer Dashboard
11. Coupons
12. Reviews & Ratings
13. Wishlist
14. SEO Management
15. Analytics Dashboard
16. Email Notifications
17. Returns & Refunds
18. CMS (Pages & Blog)

This provides a solid, production-ready foundation that can later be extended with advanced capabilities like subscriptions, loyalty programs, AI-powered recommendations, and multi-vendor support.
